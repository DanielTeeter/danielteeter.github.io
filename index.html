Lessons

Many yachting and sailing clubs offer sailing lessons, from basic introductory information to racing skills. Build your skills in small "razor" sailboats, graduate to 24-foot sailboats, and you can even join a competitive offshore racing team.

Certifications

Certifications are extremely important because they show you have the skills and knowledge to safetly sail various sizes of boats. Certifications come in many type and take anywhere from a weekend to several years to earn. We highly recommend them and have excellent instructors and evaluators.

Racing

People have been sailing the world for milenia, and racing has been at the core of all demonstrations of skill. When you begin racing, you learn the thrill of competition, satisfaction of teamwork, and the love of sailing. There are countless races throughout the world every year at every boat class. Learn how to become part of a crew and how racing adds unparalleled excitement to life.
